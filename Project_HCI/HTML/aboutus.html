About Us

Our History

ERamen is a renowned worldwide ramen restaurant that originated in Japan in 1910. With a rich history spanning over a century, ERamen has established itself as a prominent name in the culinary world, specializing in the art of creating delicious ramen noodles. In 2015, ERamen expanded its reach to Indonesia, introducing its authentic and flavorful ramen to the Indonesian market.

Our Vision

The vision of ERamen is to offer a memorable dining experience to customers by serving top-notch ramen dishes that showcase the rich flavors and traditional craftsmanship of Japanese cuisine. ERamen strives to create an inviting atmosphere where customers can immerse themselves in the authentic taste and culture of Japan, fostering a sense of nostalgia and appreciation for the art of ramen-making.

Our Mission

The mission of ERamen revolves around three core principles: quality, innovation, and sustainability. ERamen is committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality in every aspect of its operations, from sourcing ingredients to the cooking process. The company continuously seeks innovative ways to enhance its menu, introducing new flavors and ingredients while staying true to the essence of traditional ramen.
